Got Questions?

We've answered the most common questions. Still need help? We're always just a message away on WhatsApp!

Ask Us Anything
How do I place an order?
+
Ordering is simple! Just send us a message on WhatsApp at 0257 626 824 or find us on Snapchat at dottyclera23. Tell us what you're looking for, your size, and we'll guide you through the rest.
Do you offer delivery?
+
Yes! We offer delivery across Ghana. Delivery fees and timelines vary depending on your location. Message us on WhatsApp for delivery details specific to your area.
What condition are the items in?
+
All our items are carefully hand-selected and inspected for quality. We only stock pre-loved pieces that are in excellent condition — clean, well-maintained, and ready to wear.
How do I know my size will fit?
+
When you message us, share your measurements or usual clothing size and we'll help you find the perfect fit. We take sizing seriously and want you to love every piece you receive.
Can I see more items before buying?
+
Absolutely! Follow us on Snapchat at dottyclera23 to see new arrivals. You can also message us on WhatsApp and we'll share photos of specific items you're interested in.
What payment methods do you accept?
+
We accept Mobile Money (MTN MoMo, Vodafone Cash, AirtelTigo Money) and cash payments for local pickups in Adenta. Message us on WhatsApp to confirm payment options for your order.
